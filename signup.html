Sign Up For Toucan Music Lessons

We offer free group lessons and affordable private lessons

Private Toucan Music Lessons are a great way to learn music as our teachers will be individually focused on training your child with no others in the way.

Note that private lessons will be 60$ for one year and a dollar a lesson after that. You can reschedule the lesson anytime.

Private learners are allowed to take home their Toucan issued instruments home. Repairs for the instrument are free. However, if you take group lessons, you cannot bring your instrument home. Please mention if you are signing up for group lessons or private lessons.
